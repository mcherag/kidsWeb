Deep Dish Pizza

This style of pizza is famously associated with the city of Chicago, Illinois. They call it deep dish because it's deep! Sometimes it seems more like a soup than pizza

Matzah Pizza

This pizza uses matzah, a kosher kind of flatbread used in Jewish cuisine. Because Jews are forbidden from eating leavened bread during Passover, some individuals use matzo as a substitute for traditional pizza crusts during the holiday.
